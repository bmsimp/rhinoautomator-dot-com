The Archetypes of Automation Framework

Developed for the talk "Building Automation Muscle Without a Dev Team", this framework helps MSP/ITSP teams identify their collective strengths and gaps across 8 dimensions of automation capability.

The core insight: the biggest barrier to automation in small MSP teams is not technical skill — it is process thinking, team composition, and role awareness. No single person covers all archetypes. Teams need to assess collectively and identify gaps to guide hiring, training, or partnership decisions.

How to Use This Tool

Individual: Score yourself 0–10 on each archetype. Use the sub-archetype descriptions to calibrate. Click Export to save your results as a JSON file so you can return later.

Team: Each team member completes their individual assessment, clicks Add to Team, then exports the combined team file. Anyone can import a team file to view the shared radar and gap analysis.

Reading the Team Radar

Each team member appears as a separate shape on the radar. Look for coverage, not duplication — two 9s in Building with zero in Quality is a risk profile. The Gap Analysis sorts archetypes from lowest to highest average team score, highlighting where investment is most urgent.
