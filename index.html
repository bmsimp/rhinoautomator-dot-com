// AUTOMATION ENTHUSIAST · COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT & EDUCATION

MAKING AUTOMATION
ACCESSIBLE.

Helping MSPs and IT teams build automation muscle — without needing a dedicated dev team. Practical frameworks, real workflows, and a methodology anyone can contribute to.

01 — What I Do

THREE PILLARS

Not a dev shop. Not a vendor. A practitioner who builds automation and teaches others to participate — regardless of technical background.

AUTOMATION

Building real workflows that solve real problems. Rewst, Power Automate, and whatever gets the job done — focused on MSP and IT operations.

CONSULTING

Helping teams identify what to automate, who should be involved, and how to build a practice that doesn't depend on a single developer.

COMMUNITY & EDUCATION

Conference talks, workshops, and community content on building automation muscle, team capability mapping, and making non-technical people meaningful contributors.
02 — The Framework

ARCHETYPES OF AUTOMATION

The biggest barrier to automation isn't technical skill. It's process thinking, team composition, and role awareness. The Archetypes framework maps 8 dimensions of automation capability so teams can find their gaps and fill them.

INDIVIDUAL SELF-ASSESSMENT

Score yourself across 8 archetypes to understand where you contribute most and where you have room to grow.

TEAM RADAR CHARTS

Overlay individual scores to visualize your team's coverage. Two 9s in Building and zero in Quality is a risk profile.

GAP ANALYSIS

The archetype with the lowest combined team score is your most urgent investment in hiring, training, or partnerships.

RESEARCH-BACKED

Built on established frameworks: Theory of Constraints, Lean, Design Thinking, Kotter's Change Model, Team Topologies, and more.

READY TO FIND YOUR AUTOMATION STRENGTHS?

Take the Archetypes of Automation self-assessment! It takes about 10 minutes and gives you a clear picture of where you and your team stand.
