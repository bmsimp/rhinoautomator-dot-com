CRACKING THE AUTOMATION CODE: HOW PROCESS-MINDED THINKERS CAN THRIVE IN TECHNICAL ROLES
Synopsis

What if your most valuable asset in building an Automation Center of Excellence isn’t advanced coding skills, but your natural ability to spot patterns, connect systems, and ask the right questions? In this session, two unconventional automation experts—one process-centric and the other from a non-traditional technical background—share how their unique approaches create robust, human-friendly workflows. Drawing on real-world experiences, we’ll debunk the myth that deep programming is required for automation success. Instead, learn how leveraging curiosity, creativity, and process-oriented thinking can lead to rapid, effective automation wins.
