## Team Assessment workflow:
1. Collect individual assessments from each team member
1. Each person completes their own assessment on the Individual Assessment tab and clicks **+ Add to Team Assessment**
1. Or share exported `.json` files with a facilitator who imports them via the Individual Assessment tab

## Interpreting Team Results
1. Where radars diverge on an archetype, that disagreement is worth discussing.
1. Where all evaluators score an archetype low, that is your most urgent gap.
1. The totals for each archetype are displayed in the Gap Analysis card from lowest to highest score.
