## Individual Assessment Workflow:
1. Open the assessment tool in your browser
1. Enter the name of the person being assessed (e.g. "Jane Smith, Operations Lead")
1. Score their capability 0–10 on each archetype — expand each section and ask: *does this person demonstrate these characteristics?*
1. Click **+ Add to Team Assessment** to include them in the team view once you've completed your review of all archetypes.
1. Repeat for each team member.

## Saving Individual Results for Reuse
1. Click **Export** to save the assessment as a `.json` file

## Importing Prior Individual Results
1. Click **Import**
1. Locate your `.json` file and open.
1. Click **+ Add to Team Assessment** to include them in the team view.
